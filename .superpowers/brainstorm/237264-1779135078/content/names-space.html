Apogee

Peak of orbit · clean · confident

The highest point in an orbit — where you have full visibility over everything below. Strong metaphor for a tool that gives you complete oversight of your library. Sounds like a real product without being obvious.

Pulsar

Neutron star · scanning energy · punchy

A pulsar emits regular, precise pulses — perfect for a tool that scans, checks, and monitors. Short and punchy, sounds technical without being jargon. Has a satisfying weight to it.

Vega

Bright star · 2 syllables · immediately space

Fifth brightest star in the sky. Short, clean, and unmistakably space without needing any explanation. Sits in the same register as Plex or Sonarr — just a name that sounds right.

Telemetry

Telemetry is the data continuously transmitted from a spacecraft — codec, bitrate, duration, corruption status. Captures exactly what the tool does: monitoring and reporting on your media. Longer, but distinctive and memorable.

Probe

Space probe · scan + explore · short and direct

A space probe ventures into unknown territory and reports back what it finds. Doubles as exactly what the scanner does — it's literally calling ffprobe under the hood. Very short, very direct, completely unambiguous.

Orbit
