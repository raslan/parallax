Parallax Shift

Three stars — the central bright one and two "ghost" positions showing the parallax effect directly. With the measurement arcs it reads immediately as an astronomical instrument. Abstract but accurate to the concept.

Reticle / Telescope

Classic telescope crosshair — the view through an eyepiece when you're measuring stellar positions. Immediately reads as "precision instrument" and works at small sizes. The most recognisable of the three; what you'd see on a mission control screen.

P Lettermark

A clean monospaced "P" with a small star accent — the simplest mark, works at every size from favicon to large display. Feels like the logo of a proper software tool. Easy to reproduce in any context; less conceptually rich but maximally legible.
